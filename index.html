What is a cat?

Catshave sharp, retractable (except in the cheetah) claws, and their teeth are adapted for stabbing, anchoring, and cutting. They almost always land on their feet when they fall from a height

Breed of Cats

Ragdoll

Ragdoll is a breed of cat with a distinct colorpoint coat and blue eyes. Its morphology is large and weighty, and it has a semi-long and silky soft coat.

British Short

British Short Hair is the pedigreed version of the traditional British domestic cat, with a distinctively stocky body, thick coat, and broad face. and it has a semi-long and silky soft coat.

Exotic Short

Exotic Short Hair is a breed of cat developed as a short-haired version of the Persian. The Exotic is similar to the Persian in appearance with the exception of the short dense coat.

Bengal

Bengal cat is a breed of hybrid cat created from crossing of an Asian leopard cat, with domestic cats, especially the spotted Egyptian Mau.
